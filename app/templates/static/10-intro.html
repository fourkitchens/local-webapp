Let’s cut to the chase

We know you’re reading a lot of these, and judging by the heft of our own proposal, it’s not an easy job. So to make this process easier for you, we’d like to pare down the key facts into a single, handy-dandy page.

Our vision: Reinforce the Microfinance Gateway’s position as the most comprehensive web resource for the global microfinance community by designing a site worthy of international attention.

As an interactive hub of information exchange, the new Microfinance Gateway site will be an invaluable resource for those involved in the microfinance community, empowering all with relevant, timely, and actionable knowledge. Four Kitchens will work closely with CGAP to design the information architecture, wireframes, user experience across all devices, and technical and nontechnical requirements for merging the four Gateway sites into a single Drupal platform and database, all while allowing editorial independence for the regional sites, supporting existing languages, and laying a strong foundation to easily expanding language support in the future.

We at Four Kitchens are very excited about this project!

The user-centric, responsive design and multilingual challenges facing the new Microfinance Gateway site is exactly the rich blend of strategy-based solutions, technical innovation, and social good we thrive on. Like you, we are passionate idealists. Four Kitchens’ stated mission is to change the world by setting knowledge free. We do this by working with open-source software, contributing back to open-source and free-culture communities, and choosing projects that further the spread of knowledge and ideas. The Microfinance Gateway is precisely the kind of project that excites us: It’s utilizing open-source technologies to free knowledge that will change the world.

We share your bold and ambitious vision for this project, and we hope to forge a long-term partnership in innovation with CGAP’s Microfinance Gateway.
