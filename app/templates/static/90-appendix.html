Appendix

In order to give you a better idea of how our design process works, we’ve included some assets from our redesign of ICANN.org, the main, public-facing website of the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers. (ICANN is the organization responsible for all domain names and IP addresses on the internet.) The project was a massive effort: Analyzing more than 43,000 static HTML files in six languages, performing an entire overhaul of the IA, design, and multilingual support, and moving the entire property to a responsive Drupal 7 website.

If you’d like to learn more about the details of the entire project, we presented a case study at DrupalCon Denver 2012 titled “Big websites for small screens.”
